About Selfnet e.V.

Selfnet e.V. is a student-run non-profit focused on operating reliable networks for student housing and on sharing operational knowledge. We connect students, academia, and industry to advance practical networking skills.

What we do
  • Campus and housing internet
  • Workshops and talks
  • Open-source operations
